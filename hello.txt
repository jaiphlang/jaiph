**Fun fact:** **Jakub Błaszczykowski** (“Kuba”) is a Polish former footballer 
who played for Borussia Dortmund and the Polish national team. He’s one of the 
most capped Polish players of all time and was known for his speed and work 
rate on the wing. He also set up the **Kuba Foundation** to support children 
from difficult backgrounds, partly inspired by his own tough childhood. So 
there’s at least one well-known Jakub who turned hardship into helping others.
